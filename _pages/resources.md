## Navigation Apps & Maps
### Primary Apps
- **FarOut (formerly Guthook)**
  - Most popular among AZT hikers
  - Detailed waypoints
  - User comments and updates
  - Offline capability
  - Water source updates
  - $14.99 for AZT guide

- **Gaia GPS**
  - Excellent topo maps
  - Track recording
  - Offline functionality
  - Weather forecasts
  - $39.99/year premium
